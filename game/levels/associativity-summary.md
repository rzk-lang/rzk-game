title: Wrap-up
---

You lifted composition into the arrow type, composed the witnesses there, and read the triple composite off the main diagonal of the resulting tetrahedron. Its two faces present that composite as both $(h\circ g)\circ f$ and $h\circ(g\circ f)$, so uniqueness of Segal composites makes the two bracketings equal. That is associativity. With composition that is unital and associative, a Segal type is a **pre-$(\infty,1)$-category**; strengthening it with local univalence — a **Rezk type** — gives a genuine $(\infty,1)$-category.
